## Export Notes

**SVG:** ViewBox 256×256 or natural ratio. Flatten transforms. No embedded bitmaps. Run through [SVGO](https://jakearchibald.github.io/svgomg/) to strip editor metadata.

**PNG:** Exact pixel dimensions per inventory. 72 DPI. Transparent background for icons, themed background for OG images.
